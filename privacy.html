Privacy Policy

Skanna by LiDAR3D — iOS app

Effective date: 12 July 2026

The short version: Skanna does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data. Everything you capture — scans, 3D models, photos, notes, and site locations — is stored only on your device. The app works entirely offline, has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Who we are

Skanna by LiDAR3D ("Skanna", "the app") is developed and published by Anders Olofsson ("we", "us"). This policy explains what the app does — and does not do — with your information.

Data we collect

None. We do not collect any personal data, usage data, analytics, identifiers, or diagnostics. No information about you or your use of the app is ever sent to us or to any third party. The app contains no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

What the app accesses on your device, and why

To do its job, Skanna asks for the following iOS permissions. Everything accessed through them is processed and stored on your device only:

  • Camera and LiDAR sensor — used to capture 3D scans of your spaces. Scan imagery and depth data are processed on-device to build your 3D models.
  • Location (while using the app) — used to tag your scan sites on the map so you can find them again. Site coordinates are saved only in the app's local storage.
  • Photo library — used only when you choose to attach photos to a site or save images. The app accesses only the items you pick.

You can decline or revoke any of these permissions at any time in the iOS Settings app. The related features will simply be unavailable.

Where your content is stored

All scans, 3D models, photos, panoramas, notes, and site information are stored locally in the app's private storage on your device (and in your device backups, if you have iCloud or local backups enabled — those backups are governed by Apple's terms, not ours). We have no access to any of it.

Deleting a scan or site in the app deletes its files from the app's storage. Deleting the app removes all of its data from your device.

Sharing and exporting

Skanna lets you export scans, models, and site archives (for example as GLB, OBJ, USDZ, or ZIP files) using the standard iOS share sheet. Sharing happens only when you initiate it, and the data goes only to the destination you choose. We are not involved in, and have no visibility into, anything you share.

Internet connection

Skanna works fully offline. The app does not upload your content or any other data to our servers or anyone else's. Map imagery on the site map is provided by Apple Maps (MapKit), which operates under Apple's privacy policy.

Children

Skanna is a professional tool and is not directed at children. Since the app collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

Your rights (GDPR, CCPA, and similar laws)

Privacy laws such as the EU GDPR and the California CCPA give you rights over personal data that companies hold about you. Because we do not collect or hold any personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, delete, or port. If you believe otherwise or have any privacy question, contact us and we will respond.

Future features

If a future version of Skanna adds optional online features (such as cloud storage or AI processing), those features will be opt-in, this policy will be updated first, and no content will leave your device without your explicit action.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update this page and the effective date above. Material changes will also be noted in the app's App Store release notes.
